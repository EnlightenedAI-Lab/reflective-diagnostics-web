Module 7

Absolute Intelligence

Runs several frontier models on the same question, scores them, shows them peer feedback, and synthesizes a stronger final answer.

  • Multi-Model Several models answer the same prompt
  • Judge A structured scorecard evaluates each answer
  • Revision Peer feedback is shown back to the models
  • Synthesis A stronger final answer is built from the best parts
Why it matters

A difficult question should not depend on a single model’s first answer.

What we found

Scoring, peer feedback, and revision produced a stronger final answer than any one model alone.

Methodological Contrast

Most systems evaluate AI through interpretation.

Reflective Diagnostics evaluates it through explicit, repeatable rules.

It does not use AI to judge AI. It uses structured rules to make hidden reliability signals visible.

Why it matters

Most evaluation methods can see the output, but not whether the system is actually reliable.

What we found

Across these modules, the final answer repeatedly hid risks that only structured evaluation made visible.
